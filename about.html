Our Story

More than just breakfast. It's a tradition of freshness.

Founded by Passion

Berfis Cafe was born from a simple desire: to provide Accra with breakfast and catering that feels like home but tastes like luxury. Founded by Deborah Asmua, we believe that the first meal of the day sets the tone for everything that follows.

What started as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved service known for our signature "Full Ghanaian" breakfasts and exquisite gift baskets. We don't just deliver food; we deliver an experience of care, quality, and punctuality.

Why Choose Berfis?
